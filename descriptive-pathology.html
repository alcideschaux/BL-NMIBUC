Pathologic features

This section includes the pathologic features of the 143 cases that were included in the dataset. For the histologic diagnosis, “Nontumor” includes normal urothelium and papillary hyperplasia, “CIS” includes carcinoma in situ and dysplasia, “LG” and “HG” mean low-grade and high-grade noninvasive papillary urothelial carcinoma, respectively.
